How styles are grouped into style sheets/embedded sheets

I used 5 style sheets in total.They are:
1) reset.css remove the default settings of the browsers.
2) universal.css set the universal common scene: background-image, font-family, header, footer, links, tablinks...
3) homepage.css apply on homepage and divided into different sections: information,promotion,reservation...
4) orderpage.css apply on orderpage and divided into different sections: menu,table...
5) orderprint.css apply on the print page
I used 1 embedded style sheet:
The one in design page.
Didn't use inline styles.
